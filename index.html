    Unlike conventional equipment, our device provides a low-cost, low-latency, and convenient way to examine Parkinson's disease (PD). With the device, we can detect the potential symptoms of PD by daily walking as early as possible.

    Early Prediction with High Accuracy

    We train our model by the open-source database which contains measures of gait from PD patients and normal people. Therefore, our device can predict the PD severity level of users. Moreover, the accuracy of our model is about 90%.

    Adaptability to Different Ways of Walking

    By means of data preprocessing, our device is suitable for people with all kinds of weight, speeds, and ways of walking.

    Excellent User Experience

    Users are able to get a daily checkup in only 30 seconds with the simple user interface. Also, users are allowed to create their own accounts. Individual past data can be found once you log in.
